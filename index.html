Research Highlight - Ankle Exoskeleton

Designed Lightweight Ankle-Exoskeleton for Sarcopenia-induced Mobility Impairments to reduce jerk-accleration on ankles during walking by 80%. Presented at ICBT Cardiff 2025 and accepted for publication by SpringerNature in Studies in Systems, Decision, and Control.

Project Highlight - Life in Orbit

Life in Orbit is a pedagogical game that brings ISS to classrooms. Outreached to ~3000 students improve awareness about STEM careers through improved accessibility. Actually recreated astronauts' experiences in orbit, remodelling scientific equipment and curating an engaging set of challenges.
